Welcome to Safety Room

Coaching & 1-on-1 Counseling/Therapy Sessions put together to help adolescents(ages 9-16) through the challenges of 2020 and their teenage years.

Register

Modules

  • BE: Identity & self imagery, understanding & aligning with self, making the best use of talent and strengths.
  • Emotional Stability and Mastery: Ensuring emotional wellness during a crisis, dealing with unexpected circumstances, fear, anxiety, panic attacks, confusion, pain, disappointment & hurt.
  • Mental models and constructs: Decision making & problem solving.
  • Personal counseling & therapy session: Avenue to talk, share burdens, ask questions, seek counsel and heal

    Safety room will run through four consecutive Saturdays.
    We look forward to hosting and empowering your teenagers as we work to protect their #safetyinanunsafeworld.
